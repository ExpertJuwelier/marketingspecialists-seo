What has been built so far

This SEO section now has three different page types working: a guide page, an industry page, and a city page. That means the site is starting to behave like a real SEO content system rather than a single test page.

Why that matters

Digital Marketing FAQs

How long does SEO take?

SEO is a long-term channel. Some improvements can happen within weeks, but stronger ranking movement usually takes a few months depending on competition, site quality, and consistency.

Can these pages generate leads?

Yes. These pages are being built to attract search traffic and then direct readers toward your services, tools, audits, and contact options.

Will tools be embedded inside articles?

Yes. Some pages can include tools directly inside the content, while others can link readers to a separate tool page depending on what works best for the experience.

Can these pages link back to my service pages?

Yes. That is one of the main reasons for building them. Each page can support your core service pages through internal linking.

Will Google Ads still work on these pages?

Yes. You can place ad blocks in the same layout once the article system is fully connected to your monetisation setup.
